SidePortal

Get the extension

SidePortal keeps your day moving by putting Gmail, Drive search, Calendar events, and organization quick links directly in Chrome’s side panel. No extra tabs. No hunting.

What it does

  • Open Gmail and see unread status at a glance
  • Search Google Drive from the side panel
  • View upcoming Calendar events
  • Save personal quick links
  • Join or create Organization Quick Links using a share code
