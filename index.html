SidePortal

SidePortal keeps your day moving by putting Gmail, Drive search, Calendar events, and organization quick links directly in Chrome’s side panel. No extra tabs. No hunting.
Get the extension Privacy Built for speed Minimal data

What it does

  • Open Gmail and see unread status at a glance
  • Search Google Drive from the side panel
  • View upcoming Calendar events
  • Save personal quick links on your device
  • Join or create Organization Quick Links with a share code

How it works

Google sign in uses Chrome Identity and Google OAuth, so the extension can request your Gmail and Calendar data only after you approve access.
Quick links are either stored locally (personal) or shared through your organization group (Supabase database).
Side panel workflow keeps you in one place while you check what matters and jump to the right page when needed.

Security and privacy highlights

No selling of data. SidePortal does not sell personal information.
Least access. Only requests the Google scopes needed for the features you use.
Local first. Personal links are stored in Chrome storage on your device.
Clear controls. You can disconnect by logging out, and you can remove the extension at any time.
SidePortal uses Google APIs to display Gmail unread status, upcoming Calendar events, and Drive search shortcuts. Organization Quick Links are stored in a Supabase database to support group sharing by code. For full details, read the Privacy Policy.
